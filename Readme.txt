## Introduction

The AI Video Creator website is designed to demonstrate the capabilities of an AI-based video creation tool. Users can leverage the power of artificial intelligence to generate compelling videos automatically from photos, making the process of creating captivating videos quick and effortless.

## Features

- AI-powered video creation from photos
- Professionally designed templates and transitions
- User-friendly interface for easy interaction
- Quick and automated video rendering process
- Option to customize video settings and effects
- Responsive design for seamless use across devices

## Getting Started

To explore the AI Video Creator website, follow these steps:

1. **Visit the Website**: Open your web browser and go to [https://pedropixell.github.io](https://pedropixell.github.io).

2. **Home**: The home page introduces the website and its purpose. Scroll down to see a captivating hero section with a call-to-action button for hiring the professional photographer.

3. **Gallery**: Click on the "Gallery" link in the navigation menu to view a collection of stunning photos. These images will be used as input to create the AI-generated videos.

4. **Gallery Single**: When you click on the "Details" link of a specific gallery item, you'll be directed to a page where you can view the image in full size and enjoy it individually.

5. **Services**: The "Services" page provides information about the video creation service, AI technology, and customizable options.

6. **About**: The "About" page offers insights into the background of the AI Video Creator project and its team.

7. **Contact**: If you are interested in hiring the photographer or have any inquiries, visit the "Contact" page and fill out the provided form.
